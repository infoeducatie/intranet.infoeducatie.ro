Treasure hunt

For those who didn't solve the easy steganography challenge, there are some hints:

  • Look more carefully at the file and spot a place good for writing interesting stuff inside it.
  • There's an interesting base64 encoded string encrypted in an usual way.
  • Extract a small power of two bits from the funny colors. Don't forget to test the bits in reverse order.

Also, do not forget to mail me if you are in trouble cmin@ropython.org and stay tuned for more hints, good luck!

  • After you solve the medium stegano challenges, show it to a Bitdefender judge and you will get the big prize.
